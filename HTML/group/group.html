About Aespa

Aespa (Korean: 에스파) is a South Korean girl group from SM Entertainment. It has four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning. Known for its metaverse concept and hyperpop music, Aespa debuted on November 17, 2020, with "Black Mamba," which set a record for debut views in K-pop. Their follow-up single, "Next Level," was a hit, earning Song of the Year at the Korean Music Awards. In October 2021, Aespa released their first EP, Savage, which topped charts in the US and South Korea. Their second EP, Girls, released in July 2022, became the best-selling album by a K-pop girl group and hit number three on the Billboard 200. Their third EP, My World, released in May 2023, sold over two million copies and also reached the top ten on the Billboard 200. In May 2024, they released their first studio album, Armageddon, with the lead single "Supernova" topping charts.

Meet the Members

Karina's Bio

Yu Ji-min (Korean: 유지민; born April 11, 2000), known professionally as Karina, is a South Korean singer and rapper. She is the leader of the South Korean girl group Aespa, formed by SM Entertainment on November 17, 2020. She is also a member of the supergroup Got the Beat, which debuted on January 3, 2022.

Giselle's Bio

Aeri Uchinaga (内永 枝利, Uchinaga Eri, born October 30, 2000), known professionally as Giselle (지젤), is a Japanese singer and rapper based in South Korea. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Aespa, formed by SM Entertainment in November 2020.

Winter's Bio

Kim Min-jeong (Korean: 김민정; born January 1, 2001), known professionally as Winter, is a South Korean singer and dancer. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Aespa which debuted under SM Entertainment in November 2020. In January 2022, she became a member of the supergroup Got the Beat.

Ningning's Bio

Ning Yizhuo (Chinese: 宁艺卓; pinyin: Níng Yìzhuó; born October 23, 2002), known professionally as Ningning (Korean: 닝닝), is a Chinese singer based in South Korea. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Aespa.
